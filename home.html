About us

My Chosen People, Inc. was founded on April 26, 2014 with 12 active Board Members. Our program is for women who are pregnant facing Substance Abuse Disorder (SUD) and women released from prison who anticipate reunifying with their children. There are not enough drug treatment residential facilities for pregnant women, and more needs to be done to protect the lives that carry the addiction.
